# Privacy Policy for Portal IQ

**Last Updated:** November 2025  
**Effective Date:** November 2025

---

## Overview

Portal IQ is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard data when you use the Portal IQ service hosted at [portal-iq.platformetrics.com](https://portal-iq.platformetrics.com).

**Key Principle:** We only collect and retain data that you explicitly provide to us. We do not track, profile, or sell user data.

---

## 1. What Data We Collect

### Data You Provide Explicitly

When you use the Portal IQ hosted service, we collect only the information you voluntarily input:

**Portal Configuration & Evaluation Data:**
- Portal selection criteria and weightings you customize
- Feature importance scores you set
- Cost parameters and budget constraints you enter
- Organization-specific notes and metadata
- Comparison results and saved evaluations
- Export preferences and report formats

**Account & Authentication (if applicable):**
- Email address (for account creation and login)
- Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)
- Username or display name
- Account preferences and settings

**Usage Metadata:**
- Timestamps of when you access Portal IQ
- Pages or features you interact with
- Session duration and frequency of use
- Browser type and basic device information (for compatibility)
- IP address (for security and fraud prevention)

### Data You Do NOT Provide

We **do not collect:**
- Sensitive organizational information you don't explicitly enter
- Financial data beyond what you input for portal cost analysis
- Employee names, contact details, or personnel records
- Internal documentation or confidential files
- Location data or geolocation tracking
- Biometric or health information

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

**Your Data is Used For:**

1. **Service Delivery**
   - Storing your portal evaluations and comparison results
   - Enabling you to access, edit, and retrieve your saved assessments
   - Personalizing the Portal IQ experience based on your preferences
   - Generating customized reports and recommendations

2. **Account Management**
   - Creating and managing your user account
   - Authenticating your identity and maintaining session security
   - Processing password resets and account recovery
   - Communicating with you about your account

3. **Service Improvement**
   - Analyzing aggregated, anonymized usage patterns
   - Identifying and fixing bugs or technical issues
   - Understanding feature adoption and user engagement
   - Improving the Portal IQ algorithm and methodology
   - Enhancing user experience and interface design

4. **Security & Compliance**
   - Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access
   - Enforcing our Terms of Service
   - Meeting legal and regulatory obligations
   - Protecting the integrity of the service

**Your Data is NOT Used For:**
- Marketing or advertising targeting
- Selling or sharing with third parties
- Creating user profiles for commercial purposes
- Behavioral manipulation or dark patterns

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

### Where Your Data is Stored

- Your evaluation data is stored on **secure servers hosted by Platformetrics Labs** (or our trusted cloud infrastructure providers)
- Data is stored in **encrypted databases** with access controls
- Backups are maintained for disaster recovery and business continuity

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices:

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data sent to/from Portal IQ is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Encryption at Rest:** Your stored data is encrypted in our databases
- **Access Controls:** Only authorized personnel can access your data, and only for legitimate service purposes
- **Authentication:** Strong password requirements and optional multi-factor authentication (if enabled)
- **Regular Audits:** Security reviews and vulnerability assessments
- **Incident Response:** Documented procedures for data breach notification and response

**We Are Not Responsible For:**
- Loss of data due to your actions (e.g., forgotten passwords, deleted accounts)
- Security breaches resulting from compromised user credentials
- Unauthorized access if you share your login credentials

---

## 4. Data Retention

**How Long We Keep Your Data:**

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Account & Profile Data | For the duration of your account; deleted 30 days after account deletion |
| Portal Evaluations & Results | For the duration of your account; you can delete anytime |
| Usage Logs & Metadata | 90 days for analytics; 1 year for security audits |
| Backups | Up to 90 days (for disaster recovery) |
| Session Data | Until logout or session expiration |

**Deletion:**
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time
- Deleted data is removed from active systems within 30 days
- Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days for recovery purposes

## 5. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

### Who We Share Data With

**We share your data only with:**

1. **Service Providers**
   - Cloud infrastructure providers (e.g., AWS, Azure, Google Cloud) who host our servers
   - Payment processors (if you purchase premium features)
   - Email service providers (for account notifications only)

   *All service providers are bound by strict data processing agreements and confidentiality obligations.*

2. **Legal Requirements**
   - Law enforcement (only with valid legal process such as a subpoena or court order)
   - Government agencies (only as required by law)
   - Protecting our legal rights or enforcing our Terms of Service

**We DO NOT:**
- Sell your data to advertisers, brokers, or marketers
- Share data with competitors or tool vendors
- Use your data for benchmarking or public reports without your explicit consent and anonymization
- Disclose your organization's evaluation results publicly

### Third-Party Links & Analytics

Portal IQ may contain links to external websites or services. We are **not responsible** for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies independently.

**Analytics:**
- We may use anonymized, aggregated analytics (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand service usage patterns
- This data cannot identify you individually
- You can opt out of analytics tracking (see section 6)

## 6. Your Rights & Choices

### Access & Portability
- You can download or export your evaluation data at any time
- You can request a complete copy of your account data in a standard format

### Correction
- You can update, correct, or modify your account information anytime

### Deletion
- You can delete your account and all associated data

- **Email Communications:** Unsubscribe from non-essential emails via the link in each email

If you access Portal IQ from outside the region where our servers are located:

- We comply with applicable data protection laws (including GDPR for EU users)

- You have rights to access, rectification, erasure, and data portability

**For California Users (CCPA):**
- You have the right to know, delete, and opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data, but you can opt-out of analytics)
- To exercise these rights, contact: [privacy@platformetrics.com]
